Create & Save Business Cards
Create Business Card
To create a new business card, you must first be logged in to the App. Click the Menu tab on the right, and click the New Business Card link. Click the Menu tab again to hide the menu. Enter the business card owner information in the Build Card form area (See Figure #1). As you are typing the business card information, the Review Card area on the right is updated with the information you enter so you can see how your business card will look. You can continue to add and/or change information in the Build Card area. Note that the formatting for your company is already preset in the Review Card area.

Save Business Card
When you are finished entering your card data and you are satisfied with your card, click the Save Card button at the bottom of the Build Card menu to save your business card.

Getting Help: In the Build Card area, most of the form field items have context-sensitive help and tips located in the small blue icons. Hover over these icons with your mouse/cursor to get an online popup menu with help and examples about the adjacent item. Place your cursor over to view an example in-line tip.

Note: If you need to start from fresh and clear all data in the Build Business Card area, click the Menu tab and New Business Card link again and all form data will be cleared.

Edit & Order Business Cards
Edit Business Card
To edit an existing business card and/or to place an order for business card printing, click the Menu tab on the right and then click the Edit/Order Business cards link. A listing of your business cards (as well as all cards from your company or companies--Administrators only) is displayed in the left My Cards menu area. Click the Card Owner Name link to edit/order a card (see Figure #2). At the bottom of the View Card area, there are three buttons with the following possible Functions and 2 different Cases:

Case 1 This case is when the selected business card has not yet been ordered for printing; or, the order has already been completed and printed. All card details are available for editing and saving.

Edit Card  Order Card  Print Card

Edit Button: Click this button to edit card holder information for the displayed Business Card.

Order Button: Click this button to place an order for business card printing (see Order Card below).

Print Button: Click this button to see a print view of the selected business card.


Case 2 This case is when the selected business card has already been ordered and is currently scheduled for printing. Card details are NOT available for editing until after the card has been printed.

Edit Card  Review Order  Print Card

Edit Button: Becasue your card is currently being printed, you cannot edit your card at this time.

Review Order Button: Click this button to review your business card order. If your order has not yet printed, you can edit the order (see Review & Modify Existing Orders in next section for instructions).

Print Button: Click this button to see a print view of the selected business card.

Figure #2: Edit & Order Business Cards



Order Business Card
To place and order for your business card printing, follow the procedures above by clicking on the Business Card Owner Name link in the left menu area. Beneath the View Card area, click the Order Card button and complete the order information (see Figure #3).

Ordering Instructions
  1. Quantity Ordered: Type in the number of cards you want printed. Mimimum is 250 cards. Use numbers only.
  2. Contract: From the drop down list, select the Contract Name associated with your order. If you do not see your contract listed, select Other or None.
  3. Send Printed Cards Here: Type in your mailing (postal) address where you want your business cards to be mailed to. Include your name or C/O, street address, room or box number (if any), city, state and zip code.
  4. Approve Order: If you are an administrator, you will see this box, if you are NOT an administrator, you will NOT see this box. For administrators, please either select Yes or No to approve or disapprove the order.
Important: When finished making your order, please review it to ensure it is accurate. Once accurate, click the Place Order button to place your order for Business Card printing. Your order will now be scheduled for printing. If at any time before your order is printed you need to modify your order, follow this instructions in the Review & Modify Existing Orders below.

Modify & Review Orders
Modify Order
If your existing business card order has not yet been printed, you can make modifications to your order including the following four areas: 1. Quantity Ordered; 2. Approved (Yes/No) (administrator only); 3. Mail Cards To address; and 4. Contract Information. To modify an existing order, follow these instructions (also see Figure #4):
  1. Click the Menu tab on the right of the screen to show the menu selections.
  2. Click the Review Orders link in the Menu tab.
  3. Click the Business Cardholder name in the My Current Orders menu list on the left of the screen to display the order.
  4. Click the Edit button beneath the Order Details preview area in the middle of your screen. This button is beneath your order details and above your business card preview.
  5. Edit the order information as needed.
Review Order
When you have finished editing the order, make sure to review the details you edited, and if correct, click the Save Order button to save your edits. You business card order is now modified and scheduled for printing.
Note: If during your order modification, you set the Approved status to No, then your card is modified when you save the card, but the card is NOT scheduled for printing. Only Approved cards will be scheduled for printing.

Re-Ordering Business Cards
View & Select Business Card
To view and select an existing businness card for re-ordering, follow the steps below (see Figure #5):
  1. Click on the Menu tab on the right of the screen to open the menu.
  2. Click on the Review Orders link in the menu tab
  3. On the Review Orders page, click the Show Completed Orders link in the left hand My Completed Orders menu.
  4. To select a completed order, click the the selected Cardholder name to display the Business Card in the Order Details area.
Re-order Business Card
To re-order the selected Business Card, click the button at the bottom of the Order Details area. Complete all the Order details as described in the ordering steps below (Review Topic 4 if you need more assistance).
Ordering Steps
  1. Quantity Ordered: Type in the number of cards you want printed. Mimimum is 250 cards. Use numbers only.
  2. Contract: From the drop down list, select the Contract Name associated with your order. If you do not see your contract listed, select Other or None.
  3. Send Printed Cards Here: Type in your mailing (postal) address where you want your business cards to be mailed to. Include your name or C/O, street address, room or box number (if any), city, state and zip code.
  4. Approve Order: If you are an administrator, you will see this box, if you are NOT an administrator, you will NOT see this box. For administrators, please either select Yes or No to approve or disapprove the order.
Note: In the View & Re-Order Completed Orders page, a view of the business card for the order selected is displayed below the Order Details. This is an archived view of the card. If you need to edit your card, follow the instructions in Edit Card above. Please note, completed orders will always retain the archive version of the business card that was printed.

Administrative Functions
Approving Orders
If you are an Administrator for your Company, your primary responsibility on the App is to approve business card orders made by your staff on this App. By default, all business card orders made by users are NOT approved until an Administrator approves each order.

Follow the steps below to View & Approve Orders (see Figure #6):
  1. Click the Menu tab on the right to open the menu.
  2. Click the Review Orders link.
  3. Your Current Orders are listed in the left My Current Orders menu. Please Note the following:
    Order has been approved already. You can still edit the order details if you need.
    Order has NOT been approved yet. This order is Awaiting your review and approval.
  4. Click on the Order from the My Current Orders list that you want to approve/edit
  5. In the Order Details Window, click on the Edit Order button towards the bottom of the Order Details and just above the Card View.
  6. Edit the required information.
    • Number of Cards
    • Approval (Yes/No)
    • Card Mail to Address
    • Date Printed
    • Date Mailed
    • Contract
  7. When completed, click the Save Order button to save your edits. If you approved the order, the Green Check icon will show next to the Cardholder name in the My Current Orders list

Generating Reports
Build Your Report
To build your report for the printing and shipping status of business card orders, following the instructions below and see Figure #9 as an example tracking report.
  1. Click the Menu tab on the right of the screen to expand the menu options.
  2. Click the Download Orders and Reports Link in the menu tab.
  3. In the Business Cards Report area (See Figure #9), choose a start date for your tracking report by clicking on the calendar icon in the Start Date box. Note: the start date is automatically prefilled to track orders for the current week.
  4. Choose an end date for your tracking report by clicking on the calendar icon in the End Date box.
  5. Click the Build Report button to create your Order Tracking report.
  6. Once your report has been generated (usually just a few seconds), a link to your report will be displayed towards the bottom of the Business Cards Report area.
  7. Click the Report link just generated to open and download your Orders Traccking report.
  8. Your tracking report includes all the order details including the number of cards printed, the print date and shipping/mailing date. See Figure #10 for a sample report (Note: names have been obscured in this figure for privacy).

Managing App Users
Manage User
To manage user information and access to the Business Card App, click the Menu tab and then the Manage Users and Contracts tab. From the list of users, click the user you wish to manage. When you click a user, account deatils for the user are displayed in the User Details area (see Figure #11). Key items you can edit are detailed below
  1. Name/Email/Phone: type in any edits required for name, email and phone information.
  2. User Role: select the appropriate user role; move your cursor over the blue icon
    for an explanation of each user role.
  3. Online Store Access: this allows you to give or remove user access to the Business Card App online store access.
  4. Account Status: this allows you to put the account into suspended status so that the user is not allow to access the Business Card app. You can return a user to Active status at any time.
  5. User Company: if you are an administrator, this allows you to assign a user to any available company.
  6. Login User Name: the user name is set when your account is created. This cannot be changed.
  7. Password: to reset a user password, move your cursor over the blue icon
    and follow the instructions required to reset a password.
  8. Ship-to Address: these fields allow you to set a postal shipping address where a user will receive business cards. Normally, this is the users office addres, work site, or home address.
When you have completed your required user edits, click the Update User button to save your edits.

Note: If you need to delete a user, click the Delete User button. When you click the Delete User button, you are presented with a confirmation message. To complete the Delete, click OK, to stop the Delete, click Cancel. Note: if you delete a user, there is NO undo; the user deletion is final.
